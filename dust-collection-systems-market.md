<p><ul><li>Mechanical Collection Systems</li><li>Electric Collection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dust collection systems are essential for maintaining air quality in industrial environments, primarily categorized into mechanical and electric collection systems. Mechanical collection systems utilize physical methods such as filters, cyclones, and inertial separators to capture dust particles from the air. In contrast, electric collection systems employ electrostatic precipitation technology to attract and capture airborne contaminants through charged surfaces. Both systems are designed to enhance operational efficiency and worker safety by minimizing airborne dust exposure in various industries.</p></p>
