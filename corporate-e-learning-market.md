<p><p>The corporate e-learning market is primarily divided into two deployment types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise deployment involves installing learning solutions directly on company servers, allowing for greater control over data security and customization, but requiring significant IT resources. In contrast, cloud-based deployment offers access to e-learning platforms via the internet, facilitating scalability, lower initial costs, and simplified maintenance. This flexibility makes cloud solutions appealing to organizations seeking rapid implementation and remote accessibility for their employees.</p></p>
