<p><p>The arrhythmia treatment market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Medication**: Antiarrhythmic drugs are prescribed to manage heart rhythm disorders.</p><p>2. **Cardioversion**: A procedure that uses electrical shocks to restore a normal heart rhythm.</p><p>3. **Catheter Ablation**: Involves inserting catheters to destroy cells causing abnormal heart rhythms. </p><p>4. **Pacemaker**: A device implanted to regulate heartbeats.</p><p>5. **Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD)**: A device that monitors heart rhythms and delivers shocks if dangerous arrhythmias occur, ensuring proper heart function.</p></p>
