<p><p>A Data Management System (DBMS) is software that facilitates the creation, manipulation, and management of databases. The market is primarily divided into two types: relational and non-relational DBMS. Relational DBMS uses structured query language (SQL) and organizes data into tables with predefined relationships, suitable for complex queries and transactions. Non-relational DBMS, often termed NoSQL, accommodates unstructured data and provides flexibility in data storage and retrieval, making it ideal for handling diverse datasets and real-time web applications.</p></p>
