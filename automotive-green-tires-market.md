<p><p>The automotive green tires market features two primary types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires, characterized by their ply layers arranged diagonally, offer improved durability and cost-effectiveness but may have higher rolling resistance. Radial tires, with their plies arranged perpendicularly to the direction of travel, provide better fuel efficiency, enhanced handling, and longer tread life. The shift towards eco-friendly materials and manufacturing processes in both tire types emphasizes sustainability, aligning with the growing demand for environmentally conscious automotive products.</p></p>
