<p><p>The Monolithic Photonic Integrated Circuit (PIC) market comprises two primary types: Passive and Active Photonic Integrated Circuits. Passive PICs are built using optical components, such as waveguides and filters, that manipulate light without the need for external power. They are commonly used in applications like optical communications and sensors. In contrast, Active PICs incorporate components like lasers and modulators, requiring electrical input to function. They play a crucial role in advanced communication systems, enabling data transmission at high speeds and efficiency.</p></p>
