<p><p>The airplane solenoid valves market consists of two primary types: 2-way and 3-way solenoid valves. </p><p>2-way solenoid valves are used for on/off control in a single fluid path, enabling the quick regulation of fluid flow in various aircraft systems. In contrast, 3-way solenoid valves manage fluid flow across two outlets, allowing for more complex routing and control applications, including exhaust and intake functions. Both types are essential for precise operation and reliability in aerospace applications.</p></p>
