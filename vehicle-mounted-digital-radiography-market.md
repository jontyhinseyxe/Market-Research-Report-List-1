<p><strong>The Vehicle mounted Digital Radiography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle-mounted digital radiography market primarily features two types of systems: CR (Computed Radiography) and DR (Digital Radiography). CR tech utilizes imaging plates that capture X-rays, which are then digitized using a scanner, making it suitable for mobile applications where space is limited. In contrast, DR tech employs flat panel detectors for immediate digital image capture, allowing for faster processing and real-time imaging. Both systems enhance the capability of mobile medical units to perform efficient diagnostics in various environments.</p></p>
